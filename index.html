Is this economical?

Yes — the unit economics are strong. At $1,000/month ($33/day), cost per acquired golfer is ~$8.33 against $70 revenue, an 8.4× ROAS. Even with a conservative 3% CVR and $3.00 CPC, ROAS stays above 5×.

Repeat play dramatically improves returns. A golfer who comes back just 3 more times in the year is worth $280 total. Use the "Repeat visits" slider to model this — it's the strongest argument for running this campaign.

Recommended budget: $800–$1,200/month. Start at $1,000/mo. After 60 days of data, optimize toward keywords delivering the lowest cost per golfer. Don't cut budget before conversion tracking is in place.

Key risk: Without conversion tracking linked to actual tee time bookings (GolfNow, TeeSnap, or direct), you can't validate CPG or CVR. Set this up before launch.
